Who are we?

Quark Scholastic Aptitude Test (qSAT) is a national level Aptitude test organized by the student fraternity of BITS PILANI K.K. Birla Goa Campus .Started in 2014, with a participation of 500 students, we have expanded our reach to cities all across India, getting an overwhelming turnout of 2000+ Students last year.

Pattern of Conduction

Quark Scholastic Aptitude Test (qSAT) is conducted on two levels- level 1 for Classes 8-10 and level-2 for Classes 11 & 12. It involves no syllabus or general knowledge and relies solely on the student's logical and analytical thinking abilities. The question paper will have various sections like maths, puzzles, code crunchers etc. Each section has its own marking scheme. The exam is of 2 hour length .These questions are designed in a way to properly check the intellect and problem solving abilities of the participants.

Get Rewarded for your talent!!!

The meritorious students will be awarded with prizes worth INR 20,000 per city (INR 10,000 per level) and certificate of excellence by Quark 2018, BITS Pilani KK Birla Goa Campus. Also, they will be invited to visit BITS Pilani KK Birla Goa Campus and witness Quark 2017. Apart from this, exciting merchandises will be given by Quark 2018 to the students who perform exceptionally from each city. All the participants will be awarded with e-certificates of participation. A city-wise and a national merit list will be released so that the students can self-evaluate themselves.
